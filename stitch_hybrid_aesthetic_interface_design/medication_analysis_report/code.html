Patient Context

science What it is

Pantoprazole is a medication that reduces the amount of acid produced in your stomach. It belongs to a class of drugs known as proton pump inhibitors (PPIs).

assignment_ind Why you are taking it

Prescribed for the treatment of Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) and to promote healing of erosive esophagitis.

warning Note: Take 30 minutes before a meal for optimal effectiveness.

Clinical Intelligence

psychology Mechanism of Action

AI Confidence: 99%

Selectively and competitively inhibits HMG-CoA reductase, the rate-limiting enzyme that converts 3-hydroxy-3-methylglutaryl-coenzyme A to mevalonate, a precursor of sterols, including cholesterol.

speed Pharmacokinetics

Absolute bioavailability is approximately 14%. Extensive first-pass metabolism in the gastrointestinal mucosa and liver. Primary active metabolites contribute to ~70% of circulating inhibitory activity.

warning_amber

Interaction Analysis

Clarithromycin SEVERE

Concurrent use significantly increases Atorvastatin exposure (AUC increases by ~4.5 fold) due to CYP3A4 inhibition. Increased risk of myopathy and rhabdomyolysis.

Grapefruit Juice MODERATE

Consumption of large quantities (>1.2 liters daily) can increase plasma concentrations of atorvastatin.

Pantoprazole 40mg

Generic for Protonix

Product Identification

Clinical Details

Dosage 40 mg
Form Enteric-Coated Tablet
Drug Class Proton Pump Inhibitor (PPI)
Route Oral

Active Ingredients

science

Pantoprazole Sodium

Equivalent to 40mg of pantoprazole. Formulated to resist gastric acid degradation and release the active compound in the proximal small intestine.

Chemical Structure

C15H15F2N3O4S

Mechanism of Action

Pantoprazole is a substituted benzimidazole that suppresses the final step in gastric acid production by covalently binding to the H+/K+ ATP pump system at the secretory surface of the gastric parietal cell. This effect leads to inhibition of both basal and stimulated gastric acid secretion, irrespective of the stimulus.

auto_awesome AI Summary: It stops the stomach's acid pumps from working, reducing overall acidity to help heal ulcers and treat acid reflux.

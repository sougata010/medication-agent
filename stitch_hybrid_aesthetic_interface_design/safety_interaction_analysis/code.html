SEVERE

local_dining

Grapefruit Juice

MODERATE

Atorvastatin: Grapefruit juice inhibits CYP3A4, which can increase serum concentrations of atorvastatin, heightening the risk of myopathy and rhabdomyolysis.

Recommendation: Avoid consumption of large quantities (e.g., >1.2 liters daily) of grapefruit juice.
